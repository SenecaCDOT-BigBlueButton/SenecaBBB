How To Change Guest Account Password

  1. Click on the "Settings" button on the left side menu bar or on the top right corner of page.
  2. On "Settings" page,click on the "Change Password" button, this would bring you to "Change Password" page.
  3. Enter your account's current password and new password, and don't forget to confirm your new password.
  4. Click "Save" button to save changes, or click "Cancel" button to abort your changing.
