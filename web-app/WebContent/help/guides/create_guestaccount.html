How To Create Guest Account

  1. Click on the "Guest Account" button on the left side menu bar, this would bring you to "Create Guest Account" page.
  2. Type in your guest's first name, last name, and valid email in the guest information form.
  3. Click on the "Create" button to create account for your guest.
  4. Your guest should receive an email containing account username and a URL link which allow your guest to change account password!
