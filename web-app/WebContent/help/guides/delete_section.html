How To Delete Section

Note: Only System Admin and Department Admin have permissions to delete sections.

  1. Click on the "Subjects" button on the left side menu bar
  2. On "Subjects" page, you should see a table of all current course sections in system
  3. Find the section you want to remove using the "Filter entries" function by inputing relevant subject id or subject name on the "Filter entries" box, or you could click on any sortable table column header to make your search easier.
  4. Click on the remove icon on the row you want to delete.
  5. After system pops up a confirmation window, click on "Ok" to confirm your action, or "Cancel" to abort your action.
  6. If you choose "Ok", system should show you a message about your action result.
