How To Set Account Preference

Note: For guest account, you can change account password here. For student and guest user, you are NOT allow to change your nickname in BBBMan system.

  1. Click on the "Settings" button on the left side menu bar or on the top right corner of page.
  2. On "Settings" page, for all users except student and guest users, you could modify your nick name which would be displayed on the BigBlueButton conference users list.
  3. For guest user, you can change account password here.
  4. If you want to record all your event by default, you can check the "Allow event recording" checkbox.
  5. Click "Save" button to save changes, and click "Cancel" button to abort your changing.
